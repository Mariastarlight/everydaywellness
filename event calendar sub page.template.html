Seminar on Mental Health

May 30 2021 1400 - 1600 at The Downtown Conference Rooms

This is a long awaited seminar with guest speakers from Oslo Met University. The keynote speech will be delivered by PHD student, Carina Weltmann who is a specialist on mental health in the workplace. Anyone is welcome to attend and there will be a recpetion after the event for networking and mingling.
The seminar will provide guidance and advice for managing your mental health in everyday situations, and coping with challenges that are common to most people.
We are excited for this special event and we are sure all attendees will benefit greatly!
If you have any questions about this event, do not hesitate to ask. Best wishes, the EW events team.

Yoga Workshop - Inversions for Everyone!

June 12 2021 1100 - 1300 at Shala Yoga Oslo

Have you been working on your inversions? Or maybe you are a yoga newbie and feel intimidated by the prospect of heels over head poses?
Look no further, because we have the workshop for you! Teacher Matt Jonan will take you through techniques step by step to learn inversions at your level.
The key is understanding the benefits of inversions and not putting pressure on yourself to try advanced yoga poses!
Our philosophy is that the hard stuff will come naturally with practice, and we want you to have fun along the way.
A workbook and giftbag is included in the booking fee. We look forward to seeing you there!

Beach Retreat - Algarve Portugal

August 27 - September 6 2021, at The Palm Hotel, Algarve Portugal

We know you have been waiting for this announcement! This year's summer health retreat will take place in Algarve, Portugal.
A full 10 days of health inspired activities await you. This includes yoga classes, excersice classes and delicious vegen meals.
We have organised 6 out of the 10 days to include morning and evening meditation classes which we think you will enjoy.
Of course there will always be water sports available to take part in, and more information will be sent out about this in July.
The past few years we have had an enthusiastic group of 20 people each trip, and the feedback has helped us to make adjustments along the way.
This year we have secured deluxe rooms for all participants, and our organisers have put together a high value goodiebag from our sponsors.
Tickets will sell out fast, so get in touch and we'll be happy to answer your questions.
